Uqbel ara tzegreḍ abrid, muqel akka d wakka. 
Mi ara tleḥqeḍ ɣer tlemmas n ubrid, ɣiwel cwiṭ kan. 
Mi tzegreḍ abrid dayen, ur d-ttuɣal ara ɣer deffir. 
Ḥbes ɛiwed muqel. 
Mi d-tufiḍ amecṭuḥ, aderɣal, tamɣart, amɣar, ɛiwen-it ad yezger. 
Xdem-as-d abrid i tmeṭṭut yellan s tadist. 
Ur ttebdad ara ɣef yiri n ubrid, jbed akin iman-ik. 
Ur ttaǧǧa ara tarwa-ɣef yiri n ubrid. 
Mmel lwelha i win ur nessin ara tamnaḍt-nni. 
Ɛiwen aɛiban ad yezger, neɣ ad iserked takerrust-is. 
Ur zeggir ara i tkeryas d yifurguten. 
Ttwexxir ɣer rrif anda ttɛeddin medden.
Ur ttebdad ara deg tlemmas n ubrid ad tettmmeslayeḍ, ruḥ ɣer lqahwa. 
Ttqadar asaḍuf n yiberdan. 
Mi d-tnehreḍ takerrust, muqel deg lemri alemmas d yiqerniyen. 
Ur tṣuni ara mi ara d-izger uqcic amecṭuḥ, ixelleɛ yettuɣal-d d timdeffirt.
