Ad imeyyez anda iteddu. 
Tettagadem amaynut. 
Maca nessexdam allaɣ mi ara yezli wul. 
Ur ssinent ara tasertit ttawin-t kan lewhi, am wulli. 
Maca ḥemmlen tufra. 
Ayen nniḍen yettas-d iman-is.
D tamacahut yefren, drus i tt-yessnen. 
D netta i ɣef tettezzi tmacahut. 
D netta i d-yemmalen azrireg n tigawin. 
Zgan d asḥissef. 
Ala zzman i d amcafeɛ. 
Abrid n Sbiṭer tesneḍ-t, ma tebɣiḍ ad tḥadreḍ iman-im. 
Win yebɣan ad iyi-rreẓ, ma yezmer kan i lxedma. 
Aselmad n teqbaylit ɣur-s assaɣen igerzen d yinelmaden-is. 
Win ibɣan lalla, uruḥ ɣer dadda. 
Ad sentunt isegra deg lqaɛa, yiwet s ihin, yiwet sya. 
Tenna-as tmeṭṭut-nni zger-iyi-nn mafɣa ad ɣliɣ. 
Z tameṭṭut azekka ruḥ ɣer wasif, ad d-sirdemt taḍuṭ. 
Tilawin iteddun deg yiberdan, d tid ittennaɣen ɣef yizerfan-nsent. 
Llan yirgazen snen i tenhert seg temẓi. 
Llant tlawin nehrent xir n yergazen.
