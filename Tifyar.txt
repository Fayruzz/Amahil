Fɣeɣ ass-agi. 
Win yebɣan lalla, i ruḥ ɣer dadda.
Win yebɣan axelxal n ddeḥ, iwwet nneḥ. 
Akken i d as-yenna umemmay, mi d-teḥmel ad as-neṭṭes, mi tɛedda ad as-nekker. 
Semman-iyi Miḥyaf win ixedmen kra ad t-yaf. 
Ḥemmleɣ tudert, ḥemmleɣ kra yelhan deg-s. 
Nnwiɣ truḥeḍ, tilaq ddiɣ. 
Awi-d afzim, ad t-teqqen teslit. 
Taqendurt tettemles s lfuḍa. 
Aggus, yeẓḍa d isura, nbegges yes-s. 
Nteqqen timeḥremt, nrennu tamendilt ɣef tuyat. 
Amendil d lḥerma n tmeṭṭut. 
Nttarra tisfifin, d zagzag, yal wa s yini-s, yal ini d azamul.
